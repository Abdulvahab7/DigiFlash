About DigiFlash

DigiFlash is the premier student association dedicated to fostering digital literacy, innovation, and community engagement within our college.

"Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry standards through daily updates, technical workshops, and interactive quizzes."

What we do

  • Provide real-time academic announcements.
  • Organize technical seminars and hands-on workshops.
  • Host weekly quizzes to sharpen student skills.
  • Document college memories through our digital gallery.
